## Design Context

### Users
Mixed audience: students, freelancers, and team leads using the dashboard for quick, at-a-glance task status and progress.

### Brand Personality
Intelligent, sharp, minimal, and confident. The interface should feel precise and calm, with clarity first.

### Aesthetic Direction
Dark-first, minimal, and structured. Gold is the AI/active accent; navy is the surface; sky blue is the body text. The dashboard may use a subtle gradient image backdrop with a dark scrim to preserve legibility.

### Design Principles
1. Intelligent first: gold marks AI and active states.
2. Dark by default: rich black canvas with navy surfaces.
3. Functional minimalism: no decorative elements; any gradient is restrained and purposeful.
4. Soft edges, sharp thinking: fully rounded buttons/inputs, rounded cards.
5. Clarity through hierarchy: white for headlines, sky blue for body, generous spacing.
